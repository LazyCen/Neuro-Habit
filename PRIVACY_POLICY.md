# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 8, 2026**

NeuroHabit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the NeuroHabit mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Health Connect Data (Android)
NeuroHabit accesses health data via the Google Health Connect API to provide core features of the app.
- **Data Accessed**: We request read-only access to your **Step Count**.
- **Purpose**: This data is used to automatically track and mark completion of physical activity habits and to provide personalized AI-driven insights regarding the relationship between your physical activity, lifestyle, and mood.
- **Storage**: Health data retrieved from Health Connect is stored securely in our database and is associated only with your unique account.
- **Protection**: We do not sell or share your Health Connect data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. Use of information received from Health Connect will adhere to the [Health Connect Permissions Policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/11899428), including the Limited Use requirements.

### Usage Statistics (Android)
- **Data Accessed**: Screen time and app usage statistics (via `PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS`).
- **Purpose**: To help you monitor digital well-being and correlate screen time with habit consistency and mood.

### Personal Information
- **Account Data**: We store your email, username, and profile preferences (e.g., avatar) to provide a personalized experience and sync data across devices via Supabase.

## 2. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS.

## 3. Your Rights
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" option in the app's Settings.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@neurohabit.app.
